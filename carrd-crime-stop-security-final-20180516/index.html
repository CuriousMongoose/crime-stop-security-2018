Alarm Monitoring and Armed Response

When Crime Stop receives a signal from your home or business, the control room will immediately call your cell phone to verify whether you set your alarm off accidently. Whoever answers the phone is required to give a secret password stored in our records. If we do not receive an answer or it goes over to voicemail, we will immediately dispatch an armed reaction officer to see if everything is in order.

About Us

CRIME STOP originated in May 1995 due to a need in the community for crime prevention in general. This need could not be met due to shortage of manpower within the local SAPS. The main goal of the company/service at the time was Neighbourhood watch patrols.

As the success of CRIME STOP became known, the communities’ needs also increased. Currently CRIME STOP owns thirteen vehicles. We do patrol service and attend to alarms in the residential and industrial sectors. CRIME STOP also provides personal care of premises and animals during the clients’ absence from home.

Active clients are presently at ± 1050 and growing daily.

CRIME STOP offers a professional service that makes a difference in respect of prevention of crime in Heidelbergand Nigel areas conforms to the requirements as prescribed by the Security Officer’s Board (SIRA).

The Director of CRIME STOP has a security background of 24 years and was a senior officer in the South African Police service.

Crime Stop also subcontract Armed Reaction to various companies with control rooms situated outside of Heidelberg, like Coin, Chubb and SA Alarms

CRIME STOP manages security services of a very high quality and delivers a service to selected clients, to whom a HIGH QUALITY service and not just a “service“, is important.
